Everything You Need, In One Bot

Aimo brings a full studio music experience to your Discord server — for free.

Crystal Clear Audio

Stream from YouTube, Spotify, SoundCloud and more with near-zero latency.

Crystal Clear Audio High-quality music streaming powered by our own private LavaLink node. Supports YouTube, Spotify, SoundCloud, Apple Music and Deezer — near-zero latency and automatic failover.

Aimo AI

Chat with GPT-4o — music recs, bot help, or just conversation.

Aimo AI — Powered by GPT-4o Ask anything with /ask. Aimo remembers your full conversation history per user, making responses contextual and personal. Built on GPT-4o with Claude as backup — premium paid AI, not free APIs.

AI Image Generation

Generate photorealistic or anime-style images with a single prompt.

AI Image Generation Use /imagine with Premium for enhanced AI images — automatic prompt boosting and model selection between Flux Realism and Flux Anime. Powered by paid high-quality AI APIs.

Playlists & Favourites

Save, share, import, and load custom playlists. Synced to your account.

Playlists & Favourites 12 playlist commands let you create, rename, share, import, and manage playlists. Favourites let you save individual songs. Everything is tied to your Discord User ID.

Advanced Audio Filters

Bassboost, Nightcore, 8D, Vaporwave, Karaoke, Vibrato and more.

Advanced Audio Filters 10+ built-in EQ filters including 8D, Vaporwave, Karaoke, Vibrato, and Tremolo. Premium users get Bass Boost, Reverb, Stereo Widening, Nightcore, and Speed control.

Built-in Mini Games

Snake, Minesweeper, Trivia, RPS, Tic Tac Toe, and Hangman inside Discord.

Built-in Mini Games Six fully playable games using Discord buttons — Snake, Minesweeper, Trivia, Rock Paper Scissors, Tic Tac Toe, and Hangman. Challenge friends or play solo.

24/7 Mode

Aimo stays in your voice channel permanently, always ready on demand.

24/7 Mode Toggle with /247 — Aimo will stay in the voice channel even when everyone leaves. Perfect for always-on music rooms and server radio setups.

Live Lyrics

Real-time song lyrics via Genius, with full formatting support.

Live Lyrics Use /lyrics to pull up the full lyrics of any currently playing song, powered by the Genius API. Beautifully formatted and paginated for long tracks.

Lightning Fast

Slash command autocomplete, instant loading, sub-second responses.

Lightning Fast Optimised from the ground up — slash command autocomplete, instant track loading from cache, sub-second response times, and our own private LavaLink backend. Zero freezing.
