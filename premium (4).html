What Premium Unlocks

Six powerful features made for the most dedicated Aimo users.

AI Image Generation

Generate stunning AI images via /imagine. Powered by paid premium AI APIs. Auto-selects Flux Realism or Flux Anime, enhances your prompt automatically. 30s cooldown.

Speed Control

Adjust playback speed from 0.5× to 3.0× without affecting pitch using /speed. Perfect for studying, podcasts, or remix listening.

Advanced Bass Boost

Apply a deep, powerful bass boost with /boost that far surpasses the standard /filter options. Feel every beat.

Reverb & Echo

Transform any song into a spacious concert-hall experience with /reverb. A cinematic, dreamy echo effect.

Stereo Widening

Expand the audio field with /stereo for a wider, spatial soundstage. Makes every track feel like it surrounds you.

Nightcore Mode

Enable the classic Nightcore effect with /nightcore — sped-up tempo and raised pitch, the signature anime AMV sound.
