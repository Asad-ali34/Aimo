All Commands

Every command Aimo has, organised by category. Hover commands to see more detail.

13 commands — Control music playback in your voice channel

/play
Play a song or add it to the queue — supports YouTube, Spotify, SoundCloud, Apple Music links and plain search
/search
Search for songs and choose from an interactive list of results before adding to the queue
/pause
Pause the currently playing song — use /resume to continue
/resume
Resume a paused song and continue playback from where it stopped
/skip
Skip the current song and immediately play the next one in the queue
/back
Go back and replay the previous song from the beginning
/stop
Stop all music playback and clear the entire queue permanently
/disconnect
Disconnect Aimo from the voice channel and clear the session
/nowplaying
Show the currently playing song with progress bar, artwork, source, and interactive controls
/forward
Skip forward by a specified number of seconds in the currently playing track
/backward
Skip backward by a specified number of seconds in the currently playing track
/247
Toggle 24/7 mode — Aimo stays in the voice channel permanently, even when all users leave
/vcstatus
Enable or disable the voice channel status that displays what is currently playing

5 commands — Manage songs in the queue

/queue
View the full queue with pagination — see all upcoming songs with their duration and position
/shuffle
Randomly shuffle the order of all songs currently in the queue
/clear
Remove all songs from the current queue without stopping the playing song
/remove
Remove a specific song from the queue by its position number
/move
Move a track from one queue position to another to rearrange the order

11 commands — Audio settings and equalizer filters

/filter
Apply an audio EQ filter — choose from Bassboost, Nightcore, 8D, Vaporwave, Karaoke, Vibrato, Tremolo and more
/loop
Set the repeat mode: Off, repeat current Track, or repeat the entire Queue
/autoplay
Toggle autoplay — automatically queues related songs when the queue empties
/volume
Set the playback volume from 1% to 200% — 100% is the default level
/seek
Jump to a specific timestamp in the currently playing song (format: mm:ss)
/source
Set your preferred default music search source (YouTube or SoundCloud)
/speed ⭐ Premium
Change playback speed from 0.5× to 3× without affecting pitch — great for studying or remixes
/boost ⭐ Premium
Apply a deep, powerful bass boost that goes far beyond the standard filter options
/reverb ⭐ Premium
Add a reverb and echo effect for a spacious, concert-hall listening experience
/stereo ⭐ Premium
Apply stereo widening to expand the audio field and create a spatial soundstage
/nightcore ⭐ Premium
Enable Nightcore mode — speeds up the tempo and raises the pitch for the classic anime AMV sound

4 commands — Save your favourite songs to your account

/favorite-add
Add the currently playing song (or a specific URL) to your personal favourites list
/favorite-list
View all songs saved in your favourites list with pagination support
/favorite-play
Add all your favourite songs to the queue and start playing them automatically
/favorite-remove
Remove a specific song from your favourites list by selecting from your saved songs

12 commands — Create, manage, and share custom playlists

/playlist-create
Create a new empty playlist with a custom name
/playlist-save
Save the entire current queue as a new named playlist for later use
/playlist-load
Load an entire saved playlist into the queue — replaces the current queue
/playlist-append
Append all songs from a saved playlist to the current queue without clearing it
/playlist-list
View all of your saved playlists with their names and track counts
/playlist-delete
Permanently delete one of your saved playlists
/playlist-rename
Rename one of your existing playlists to a new name
/playlist-share
Generate a shareable import code for any of your playlists
/playlist-import
Import a playlist from a share code sent by another Aimo user
/playlist-track-add
Add a single song directly into one of your existing playlists
/playlist-track-list
Browse all tracks saved inside a specific playlist
/playlist-track-remove
Remove a specific track from one of your playlists

2 commands — AI chat and image generation

/ask
Chat with Aimo AI powered by GPT-4o — music recommendations, bot help, general knowledge. Remembers your full conversation history per user. Type "/ask reset" to clear history.
/imagine ⭐ Premium
Generate enhanced AI images with automatic prompt boosting and model selection (Flux Realism or Flux Anime). Powered by paid premium AI APIs. 30-second cooldown per user.
