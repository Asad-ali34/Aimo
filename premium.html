What Premium Unlocks

Six powerful features designed for the most dedicated Aimo users.

AI Image Generation

Generate stunning AI images with automatic prompt enhancement. Auto-selects Flux Realism or Flux Anime based on your input. 30-second cooldown.

/imagine [prompt]

Speed Control

Change playback speed from 0.5× to 3.0× without affecting pitch. Perfect for studying, podcasts, slowing down complex solos, or turbo listening.

/speed [0.5–3.0]

Advanced Bass Boost

A deep, powerful bass boost that far surpasses the standard filter options. Feel every beat with punchy, room-shaking low frequencies.

/boost

Reverb & Echo

Transform any track into a spacious concert-hall experience. A cinematic, dreamy echo effect that makes every song feel massive.

/reverb

Stereo Widening

Expand the audio field with spatial stereo widening. Makes every track feel like it surrounds you — ideal for headphones and immersive listening.

/stereo

Nightcore Mode

Enable the classic Nightcore effect — sped-up tempo and raised pitch. The signature anime AMV sound, one command away.

/nightcore

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Aimo Premium.

Is Premium per-server or per-user?
Premium is linked to your Discord User ID, so it follows you across every server where Aimo is present. You don't need to purchase it separately for each server.
How much does Premium cost?
Premium pricing is custom and handled directly through our Discord server. Join the support server and speak to an admin to find out the current pricing.
Does Premium expire?
Premium duration depends on the agreement made through our Discord. Some users receive lifetime access, others receive time-limited access. Details are discussed during the request process.
Can I use /imagine without a cooldown?
Premium users have a 30-second cooldown per image generation. This is in place because /imagine uses paid AI APIs with per-request costs. The cooldown ensures fair use for all Premium members.
Can Premium be transferred to another user?
Premium is non-transferable — it's linked to your specific Discord User ID and cannot be shared or transferred to another account. Contact an admin if you need to change the associated account.

Ready to Unlock Premium?

Join our Discord support server to request Premium access. Our team will get you set up quickly.
