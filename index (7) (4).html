Everything You Need, In One Bot

Aimo brings a full studio music experience, AI assistant, mini games, and live lyrics to your Discord server — completely free.

Crystal Clear Audio

Stream from YouTube, Spotify, SoundCloud and more with near-zero latency.

Crystal Clear Audio High-quality music streaming powered by our own private LavaLink node. Supports YouTube, Spotify, SoundCloud, Apple Music and Deezer — near-zero latency and automatic failover.

Aimo AI Assistant

Chat with our AI — music recs, bot help, or just conversation. Context-aware.

Aimo AI Assistant Ask anything with /ask. Aimo remembers your full conversation history per user, making responses contextual and personal. Our AI is powered by industry-leading language models with an intelligent backup system.

AI Image Generation

Generate photorealistic or anime-style images with a single prompt via /imagine.

AI Image Generation Use /imagine for stunning AI images — automatic prompt enhancement and smart model selection. Supports photorealistic and illustration styles.

Playlists & Favourites

Save, share, import, and load custom playlists. Synced to your Discord account.

Playlists & Favourites 12 playlist commands let you create, rename, share, import, and manage playlists. Favourites let you save individual songs. Everything is tied to your Discord User ID.

Advanced Audio Filters

8D, Vaporwave, Karaoke, Vibrato, Bassboost, Nightcore, Reverb and more.

Advanced Audio Filters 10+ built-in EQ filters including 8D, Vaporwave, Karaoke, Vibrato, and Tremolo. Premium users unlock Bass Boost, Reverb, Stereo Widening, Nightcore, and Speed control.

Built-in Mini Games

Snake, Minesweeper, Trivia, RPS, Tic Tac Toe, and Hangman inside Discord.

Built-in Mini Games Six fully playable games using Discord buttons — Snake, Minesweeper, Trivia, Rock Paper Scissors, Tic Tac Toe, and Hangman. Challenge friends or play solo.

24/7 Mode

Aimo stays in your voice channel permanently — always ready, never leaves.

24/7 Mode Toggle with /247 — Aimo will stay in the voice channel even when everyone leaves. Perfect for always-on music rooms and server radio setups.

Live Lyrics

Synced, real-time lyrics displayed as the song plays — always finds what you need.

Live Lyrics Use /lyrics to pull up the full lyrics of any currently playing song. Multiple providers with automatic fallback ensure lyrics are always found. Beautifully formatted and paginated for long tracks.

Lightning Fast

Sub-second responses, instant autocomplete, and our own private music node.

Lightning Fast Optimised from the ground up — slash command autocomplete, instant track loading from cache, sub-second response times, and our own private LavaLink backend.
